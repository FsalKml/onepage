SMART HARVESTING SYSTEM FOR BEE FARMING

Smart Harvesting System for Stingless Bee Farming is an integrated system consists of managing stingless bee honey harvesting information for three selected locations - Persatuan Penternak Kelulut Selangor, Negeri Sembilan and Terengganu. The information such are log harvesting history, location, honey weight can be stored and easily trace back and access for further analysis. Each of the stingless beehive log embedded with RFID card. Before the harvesting process begin, the beekeeper scans the log and start to harvest. After finished, the harvesting info of respective log will be sent to the cloud server. All collected honeybee then send to collection centre. The good quality of honey can be obtained by nanopore ceramic by removal of bubbles and impurities strapped in the honey during harvesting.

INTERNET OF THINGS(IOT)

By leveraging on an internet-based platform, this system integrates sensors with smart-phone which provide system alerts that notify the beekeeper of their harvesting info. This system will incorporate the existing pump with weight sensor, wireless system network, RFID system, honey collection database and system web apps. honey collection database is also necessary for the application of Malaysian Good Agricultural Practice (myGAP) certification.

HONEY BEE TRACEABILITY

This system provides traceability of the quality honeybee through a block chain technology. Traceability also means accountability, because blockchain can give a tamper-proof guarantee of a product's origin. Upon scan the QR label at the bottle, so, from here the consumer will know honeybee details history.
